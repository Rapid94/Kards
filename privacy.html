Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Kards ("we", "our", "us") operates the website kards.cc. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

When you use Kards, we may collect the following information:

  • Google Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Google Business Profile Data: With your permission, we access your business reviews, reviewer names, star ratings, and review dates through the Google Business Profile API.
  • Brand Settings: Any brand colors, logos, or preferences you configure within the app are stored locally in your browser.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your identity and connect to your Google Business Profile.
  • To retrieve and display your business reviews so you can create social media graphics.
  • To improve and maintain our service.

4. Data Storage

Brand settings and preferences are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. We do not store your Google reviews on our servers — they are fetched in real-time when you use the app.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth 2.0: For authentication and accessing your Google Business Profile.
  • Google Business Profile API: To retrieve your business reviews.
  • Google Fonts: For typography rendering.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your review data is only used within the Kards app to generate graphics.

7. Data Retention

We do not permanently store your Google data. When you disconnect your account or revoke access, all associated data is removed.

8. Your Rights

  • You can revoke Kards' access to your Google account at any time through your Google Account permissions.
  • You can clear your local brand settings by clearing your browser data.
  • You can request deletion of any data we hold by contacting us.

9. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption and OAuth 2.0 for authentication. We do not store passwords or sensitive credentials.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at kardssupport@gmail.com.
